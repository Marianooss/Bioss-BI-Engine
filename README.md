## 🏗 Architecture Decision Record (ADR-001): Off-Thread Heavy Lifting

### Context
Processing datasets exceeding **38MB+** and **296,000+ records** on the client side typically leads to "Jank" (UI freezing) due to the single-threaded nature of JavaScript.

### Decision
We implemented a **Multi-Threaded Chunking Architecture** using:
1.  **Dedicated Web Workers:** All data normalization, Fuzzy-Link merging, and baseline calculations are performed in a background thread (`/src/workers/dataProcessing.worker.ts`).
2.  **Micro-Chunk Processing:** Large datasets are processed in discretized 1,000-record chunks. Each chunk yields control back to the event loop momentarily, ensuring the **Main Thread stays at a constant 60 FPS**.

## 🛡 Data Consistency: Integrity & Healing

Before any analysis, the engine applies the **Integrity & Healing Protocol**:
*   **Fuzzy-Link Forcing:** Sanity-checks and unifies client identities. It handles numeric collision, prefix removal (e.g., `CLI-`), and name-based hashing for unresolved codes.
*   **Record Healing:** Identifies "Known Unknowns" by cross-referencing provincial patterns and determination signatures to reassign orphaned transactions to the correct client identity.
*   **Certified Curation:** Filters out invalid financial records ensuring the baseline is built on certified, high-quality data points.

## 🧠 Core Logic Documentation

### 1. MFR Risk Algorithm (Monthly Fix-Force)
The MFR engine employs a volume-first approach to detect churn before it manifests in financial reports:
*   **Phase 1: Fuzzy-Link Forcing:** Unifies disparate client identities and heals corrupted records using determination signatures.
*   **Phase 2: Baseline Alignment:** Establishes a rolling 6-month historical baseline focused on **Physical Volume (Determinations)**. This avoids noise generated by currency volatility or price adjustments.
    *   **Adaptive Thresholds:** Calculates the **Coefficient of Variation (CV)**. If CV > 30%, the engine automatically scales the sensitivity to account for inherent seasonality.
*   **Phase 3: Risk Stratification:**
    *   **Volume Delta Analysis:** Calculates `porcentaje_caida` by comparing current physical units against normalized baseline averages.
    *   **ASP Economic Impact:** Uses **Average Selling Price (ASP)** to quantify the theoretical revenue at risk (`impacto_economico`), providing a precise financial weight to activity drops.

### 🚥 Risk Dashboard (PRD v2.0)
The system categorizes clients into four critical states based on their performance delta:

| Risk Type | Threshold ($\Delta\%$) | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Silencio Total** | $\le -95\%$ | Immediate executive intervention. |
| **Crítico** | $\le -40\%$ | Commercial priority assigned. |
| **Riesgo** | $\le -30\%$ | CRM alert triggered. |
| **Alerta Temprana** | $\le -20\%$ | Active monitoring initiated. |
| **Fuga Total** | 90+ Days | Account marked for recovery/loss. |

### 📑 Audit Fix-Force Result
For executive-level reporting, BiOss generates an **Audit Integrity Report** (`AuditFixForceResult`):
*   **Health Validation:** Continuous validation of portfolio health comparing expected vs. calculated revenue.
*   **Cryptographic-Grade Integrity:** Every audit cycle generates a summary including summary sums, client mapping accuracy, and Q4 consistency checks.
*   **Variance Guard:** Rejects execution if variance thresholds (0.01%) are exceeded, ensuring zero-error reporting.

### 2. Dynamic SWOT Matrix (FODA)
A real-time mapping engine that plots clients and provinces into four quadrants based on **Revenue Delta (X)** vs. **Margin Volatility (Y)**:
*   **Strengths (Fortalezas):** High growth, high margin.
*   **Opportunities (Oportunidades):** Low revenue, high margin (Underserved potential).
*   **Threats (Amenazas):** High revenue, low margin (Operational risk).
*   **Weaknesses (Debilidades):** Low revenue, low margin (Candidates for termination).

## 🔒 Data Privacy & Pipeline: 100% Client-Side

BiOss operates on a **Zero-Trust Data Policy**. Because financial and client data in B2B is highly sensitive:
*   **Local Processing:** CSV/XLSX parsing occurs locally in the user's browser.
*   **No Data Exfiltration:** No client data ever touches a server. All analytics, insights, and PDF exports are generated at the edge.
*   **Enterprise Security:** Perfect for environments with strict data residency requirements or GDPR/HIPAA-like constraints.
